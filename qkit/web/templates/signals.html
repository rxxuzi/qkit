Variance Risk Premium

VRP = IV² − RV. The market's compensation for bearing variance risk. When high, options are overpriced; when low/negative, risk is underpriced.

SignalConditionActionStrategy
HIGH VRPz > 1.5Short volIron condor, straddle sell
LOW VRPz < −1.0Buy protectionOTM puts, VIX calls
NEUTRAL−1.0 ≤ z ≤ 1.5No action—

Pairs Trading

Cointegrated pairs exhibit mean-reverting spreads modelled as Ornstein-Uhlenbeck processes. The spread z-score drives entry, exit and stop-loss signals.

SignalConditionAction
LONG SPREADz < −2.0Buy B, short A (spread too low)
SHORT SPREADz > 2.0Short B, buy A (spread too high)
EXIT|z| < 0.5Close position (mean reverted)
STOP|z| > 4.0Cut losses (breakdown)
